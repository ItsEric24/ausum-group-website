Powering Growth Across African SMEs

Ausum Nexus Hub is the growth and innovation engine of Ausum Group — equipping African entrepreneurs with expert business advisory, sharp financial insight, and scalable systems. We incubate high-potential ventures through equity-aligned partnerships, driving bold transformation and long-term impact.

Business Model

Advisory Services (Fee-Based)

Businesses pay for expert support in:

  • Business structuring & formalization
  • Strategic planning & market positioning
  • Financial modeling & projections
  • Business plan & investment deck development
  • Grant/funding readiness assessments

Workshops & Programs (Recurring Income)

  • Paid masterclasses, bootcamps, and webinars
  • Subscription access to toolkits, templates, and digital products
  • Corporate trainings and SME capacity-building programs

Equity-Based Incubation (Long-Term Upside)

  • We select promising businesses for hands-on incubation in exchange for equity (5%–15% depending on scope).
  • Support includes Financial structure, Business strategy, Legal framework, Branding and Marketing, and Tech integration.
  • Through Ausum Fund - Access to capital

Partnered Projects & Sponsored Initiatives

  • Collaborations with NGOs, financial institutions, or development partners to run business transformation programs for MSMEs.
  • Revenue via service contracts or facilitation fees.
