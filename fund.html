The Ausum Venture Fund

The Ausum Venture Fund is a founder-first, purpose-led investment engine built to back bold African entrepreneurs solving real problems through sustainable business models. Designed as an evergreen reinvestment platform, the Fund grows alongside the Ausum Group—amplifying impact as the ecosystem thrives.
